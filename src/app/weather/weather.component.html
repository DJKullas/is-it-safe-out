Is It Safe Outside?

This tool uses government guidelines to evaluate the weather safety for children to play outside. Currently, the tool only evaluates the heat index and wind chill factor. I am currently working on adding weather alerts and more variables to further evaluate the current weather safety. Regardless of the output of this tool, always use your best judgement when deciding if it is safe for children to play outside.
